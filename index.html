About Me

I am working under the National Council of Science Museums and was posted at North Bengal Science Centre, Siliguri as a Project Coordinator. Currently serving as District Science Officer at District Science Centre Purulia. My work focuses on science communication through article writing and activity-based programs. I have published several hundred papers in multiple languages including Bengali, English, Hindi, Assamese, and Telugu.
